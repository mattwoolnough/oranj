Facebook Data Deletion Instructions

Effective Date: February 21, 2026
Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Oranj provides this page as the Data Deletion Instructions URL for the Richmond Customer Engagement App (Facebook Group -> Messenger).

1. Remove App Access from Facebook

  1. Open Facebook Settings & Privacy -> Settings.
  2. Go to Apps and Websites.
  3. Select Richmond Customer Engagement App.
  4. Click Remove.

2. Request Deletion from Our Systems

To request deletion of app-related data from our systems, email:

privacy@oranj.com.au

Include:

  1. Your full name.
  2. The email associated with your interaction (if applicable).
  3. Any identifier that helps us locate your records (for example, Page-scoped ID if known).
  4. Subject line: Facebook Data Deletion Request.

3. What We Delete

Upon verification, we delete or anonymize applicable app-related personal data, unless retention is required by law.

4. Timeline

  1. Acknowledgment target: within 72 hours.
  2. Completion target: within 30 days of a verified request.

5. Status and Questions

For status updates or questions, contact privacy@oranj.com.au.
