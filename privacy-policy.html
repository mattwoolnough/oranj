2. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, retain, and delete personal data when you interact with the Richmond Customer Engagement App and our Facebook/Messenger experiences.

3. Data We Collect

We may collect:

  1. Account/contact information (for example, name, email, and business contact details).
  2. Facebook/Messenger identifiers (for example, Page-scoped ID (PSID), page IDs, and thread IDs).
  3. Conversation content and timestamps from Messenger interactions.
  4. Lead and program-interest information (for example, stage, score, offer events, and owner).
  5. AI scoring evidence (for example, dimension scores, citations, and confidence values).
  6. Compliance/audit information (for example, consent status, approvals, policy checks, and overrides).
  7. Technical logs (for example, request IDs, errors, and webhook diagnostics).

4. How We Collect Data

We collect data:

  1. Directly from you (messages, forms, and requests).
  2. From Meta platform events when you interact with our Facebook Page or Messenger flows.
  3. From internal operator actions and workflow systems.

5. Why We Use Data

We use data to:

  1. Respond to inquiries in Messenger and provide requested resources.
  2. Run customer engagement, support, and lead workflow orchestration.
  3. Support operator tooling, analytics, and compliance controls.
  4. Detect abuse, enforce policy windows, and maintain auditability.

We do not use personal data for undisclosed purposes, unsolicited mass outreach outside platform policy constraints, or broker resale activities.

6. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)

Depending on jurisdiction, we process personal data under one or more of the following:

  1. Consent.
  2. Contractual necessity.
  3. Legitimate interests.
  4. Legal obligations.

7. Sharing

We may share data with:

  1. Meta/Facebook/Messenger services required to operate app messaging workflows.
  2. Cloud infrastructure providers for hosting, storage, monitoring, and reliability operations.
  3. AI processors used for draft/summarize/recommendation/scoring workflows.
  4. Customer support and analytics processors as configured.
  5. Legal or regulatory authorities where required by law.

We share only minimum necessary data, maintain processor/security controls, and do not permit onward resale.

We do not sell personal data to data brokers.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for stated purposes and legal/security obligations.

Category Retention Target
Account/contact, Facebook identifiers, conversation content, lead/pipeline data Active + 12 months inactive archive
AI scoring evidence, consent/compliance data, operator actions/audit logs 24 months
Technical logs 90 days (unless incident hold applies)

Verified deletion requests are completed within 30 days unless retention is legally required.

9. AI Privacy Controls

  1. AI inputs are limited to conversation excerpts and required lead metadata.
  2. High-risk personal data not required for model tasking is excluded.
  3. AI outputs are treated as assistance; human approval is required for sensitive/promotional sends.
  4. AI scores and citations are retained for accountability and audit.
  5. Opt-out and deletion flags are respected throughout AI workflows.

10. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request:

  1. Access to your personal data.
  2. Correction of inaccurate personal data.
  3. Deletion of personal data.
  4. Restriction or objection to processing (where applicable).
  5. Opt-out from promotional messaging.

Submit requests to privacy@oranj.com.au. Acknowledgment target: within 72 hours.

12. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including encryption in transit/at rest, role-based access controls, monitoring, rate limiting, and immutable audit logging for policy-sensitive actions.

13. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

14. International Transfers

If data is transferred across borders, we apply safeguards appropriate to the jurisdictions involved and as required by applicable law.
